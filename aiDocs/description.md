The problem most developer portfolios fail to solve is simple: recruiters have 60 seconds and no time to decode messy repos or generic screenshot galleries. Most portfolios either bury the work behind flashy animations or dump a wall of GitHub links that tell hiring managers nothing about how the developer actually thinks.

This site is built for tech recruiters and engineering hiring managers who need to quickly answer three questions: Can this person ship? Can they think through hard problems? Can they communicate? Every featured project is structured as a live, deployed product with a tight problem–solution–impact narrative, architecture decision logs, and a working demo—so you get the signal of a take‑home assignment without scheduling one. Built with Astro, React, and Tailwind for sub‑second load times, the site is optimized for the recruiter workflow, not portfolio awards.
